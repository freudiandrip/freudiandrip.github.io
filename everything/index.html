Candidates for Oblivion (Apport)

    FOIA - Child and Domestic Abuse Central Registry (The Data Liberation Project)

    The Central Registry is a database maintained by the Department of Defense in order to record and track incidents of domestic and child abuse within the military. This FOIA request is a collaboration with the Data Liberation Project.

  • Methods: exploratory research; reverse-engineering; search engine query optimization; technical writing.

    The Safety Net of Safety Nets: Peer-to-Peer Mutual Aid (Columbia Lede)

    An analysis performed on scraped and processed Facebook mutual aid group data contextualized by government and non-profit social programs. I was the IC (individual contributor).

  • Methods: data collection, cleaning, and processing; textual analysis and categorization; statistical analysis; data visualization; graphic design; interviewing and sourcing.

    Westminster's Top Dogs (Columbia Lede)

    A visual story analyzing the Westminster Dog Show's Group and Best in Show winners beginning in 1924, as framed by the organization's 146 year history. I was the IC.

  • Methods: data collection, cleaning, and processing; prototyping; data visualization; graphic design; front-end development; interviewing and archival research.

    Class, Sensibilities, and the Gilmore Girls Literary Canon (Columbia Lede)

    A data-driven essay on the 300+ books referenced in the Gilmore Girls 7 season run. Breadth of literary work was examined in the scope of class and social capital. I was the IC.

  • Methods: data collection, cleaning, and processing; prototyping; data visualization; graphic design; front-end development; research.

    FLYWHEEL (Columbia Lede)

    A full page graphic visualizing spin class data. This graphic was the companion for a D3 fundamentals tutorial that I ran, covering JS basics and the notorious general update pattern.

  • Methods: prototyping; data visualization; graphic design; front-end development.

  • Methods: graphic design, layout; copy editing.
